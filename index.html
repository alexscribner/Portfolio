About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Alex Scribner and I am a junior developer at Eleven Fifty Academy. I graduated from Lapel High School in 2016 and then went off to Purdue University. I started at the university in the exploratory program they have there. At the end of the year, I was still in the same postion from the beginning, I had no idea on what I wanted to pursue as a career let alone a major. So instead of wasting thousands of dollars at Purdue, I decided to stay at home and attend Ivy Tech where I was doing the same thing I was doing at Purdue except for less money.
I have always had a fascination with the designs and layouts of websites. Since I was younger, I always wanted to create and design my own website. When I heard about Eleven Fifty Academy, I immediately thought it was the best opportunity for myself. I cannot thank Eleven Fifty enough for giving me this opportunity and believing in me. This is the most excitement I have had for school in all my life and I am ready to expand my coding skills!
